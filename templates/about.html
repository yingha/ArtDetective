    User Instructions

    ArtDetective is a web application, where you can upload the artwork you like or self-made paintings to discover the art style. As bonus, you can also discover the museums and galeries where you can blow your art mind :)


    Data Resources

    The original dataset was extracted by Tan, et al. (2018) , consisting painings from WikiArt. Here, I substrated 23514 images of four art styles - Expressionism, Impressionism, Cubism, and Pop Art - for image deep learning and classification.
    The art map is made based on the information from artcyclopedia.
